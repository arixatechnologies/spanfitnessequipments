# Off-Page SEO Plan

## Local Authority

Fully complete the Google Business Profile with consistent business name, address, phone, categories, hours, products, service areas, showroom photographs, and regular posts. Link the website contact page and use UTM parameters for measurement.

Build consistent citations on reputable India and Visakhapatnam business directories, maps, chamber listings, fitness directories, and relevant manufacturer/dealer directories. Avoid bulk low-quality submissions.

## Reviews

Ask verified customers for honest Google reviews after delivery or installation. Provide a direct review link, never offer incentives for positive sentiment, and reply professionally to every review. Encourage useful details such as equipment type, location, and service experience.

## Backlinks And Partnerships

- Publish practical equipment and gym setup resources worth referencing.
- Offer guest articles to credible fitness, physiotherapy, architecture, hotel, apartment, and small-business publications.
- Develop relationships with gyms, trainers, sports academies, apartment associations, hotels, schools, and wellness studios.
- Request supplier or brand directory listings using accurate wording; do not claim official partnership without confirmation.
- Pursue local PR around showroom updates, major installations, community fitness events, and educational workshops.

## Content Amplification

Repurpose buying guides into short social posts, videos, checklists, and Google Business Profile updates. Link each asset to the most relevant guide or category rather than always linking to the home page.

Share useful resources with trainers, gym owners, architects, and project consultants. Track referral traffic and earned links. Refresh high-performing articles with new examples and internal links.

## Social Profiles

Maintain consistent identity and contact information on Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn, and relevant local platforms. Link back to the canonical website and cross-link official profiles where appropriate.

## Measurement

Track referring domains, local pack visibility, branded searches, calls, direction requests, form submissions, and assisted conversions monthly. Reject manipulative paid-link networks, automated comment links, exact-match anchor campaigns, and irrelevant directory volume.
